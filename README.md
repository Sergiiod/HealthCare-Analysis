# HealthCare-Analysis

## Business Context
The objective of this project was to analyze lenght of stay (LOS), costs of discharges and be able to compare every hospital performance and number of cases.

## Tools
- Power BI
- DAX
- Excel

## Key KPIs
- Total Discharges
- Total Hospitals
- Total Surgeons
- Average LOS days 
- Average Cost per discharge
- %Var Average LOS days

## Key Insights
- The pressence of major illnesses is low or null in every hospital from dataset.
- Hospitals with similar number of extreme illness and minor or moderete illness, have an average LOS days much higher than average.
- Cost are higher when a patient with an extreme or major illness is in hospital.

## Outcome
Delivered an end-to-end Power BI dashboard that enables hospitals administrators check performance in each hospital registered, allowing them to take actions to reduce high costs and LOS days.
